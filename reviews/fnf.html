Friday Night Funkin'

Review by Liam Mowry

This game is awesome. One of the best modern games and my personal favorite rhythm game, although it's also the only rhythym game I've ever played. The soundtrack is awesome, the mods are even better, and the game is just amazing.

Plot

Taken directly from the itch.io

Uh oh! Your tryin to kiss ur hot girlfriend, but her MEAN and EVIL dad is trying to KILL you! He's an ex-rockstar, the only way to get to his heart? The power of music...

Gameplay

It's like DDR, but for your fingers. Press the arrow key when an arrow is over another arrow. So basically, every other rhythm game.

Score:

94/100
