Super Mario Bros.

Review by Liam Mowry

Ok, let me get this out of the way: this game is a masterpiece. It was one of the NES's launch titles, it saved video games in North America, and is just an all-around great game.

Plot

In terms of plot, we have no plot. You play as Mario Jumpman Mario to save Princess Peach Toadstool.

Gameplay

You know what Mario is. It's a simple platformer with amazing level design.

Score:

96/100
